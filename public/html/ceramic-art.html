Ceramic Art

Come and experience the frantic rhythm and synergy of Boujour Valérie. The international award-winning ceramics expert from Saint-Amand-en-Puisaye, France has developed a wild, colorful display of unorthodox ceramic refining and finishing technique which culminates in a finale of an absolutely dysfunctional table for two. And the menu? A glaze soup, a fire tajine, and a sputtering cake… To end on a happy note, there is of course a break-and-bash ball! Come and see how he manipulates the forces and movements produced during clay firing, while adapting to specific kiln conditions (electric or gas). See how he creates juxtapositions of glazes and enamels on stoneware and porcelain, while also experimenting with the positioning of pieces within the kiln (by suspending, on a spindle, etc).
